Radakng

Kalimantan Barat

The Radakng house is a long house that is a traditional house for the Dayak tribe in Kalimantan Barat Province. This is the largest traditional house in Indonesia and has become a landmark for the city of Pontianak after the Equator Monument. The Radakng house has a length of 138 meters and a height of 7 meters. The location is on Jalan Sutan Syahrir, Kota Baru Pontianak.

Kalimantan Timur

The lamin house is a traditional house from the province of Kalimantan Tiokokomur. The Lamin house which is a traditional house of the Dayak tribe is known as a long and continuous stilt house and consists of many rooms. Tongkonan

Sulawesi Selatan,Toraja

Tongkonan house is the traditional house of Sulawesi Selatan from the Toraja Tribe. This house is a place to live, customary power, and the development of the socio-cultural life of the Toraja people. Tongkonan cannot be owned by individuals, but is owned communally and from generation to generation by the family or clan of the Toraja Tribe.

Rumah Balla Lompoa is a traditional house of Sulawesi Selatan which literally means a big house or an oversized house inhabited by the king. aceh

Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam

Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam (NAD) is a province located in the westernmost part of Indonesia. The traditional house of Aceh Province is Rumoh Aceh.Rumoh aceh is a stilt house that has three parts.
